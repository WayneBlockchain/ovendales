🛒 Shopping Centres & Daily Essentials

Perfect for groceries, pharmacies, banking, and everyday necessities.

Located approximately 4 km away, Northmead Square is the closest major retail hub, featuring:

  • Woolworths Food
  • SuperSpar
  • Clicks Pharmacy
  • Major bank ATMs
  • Clothing and specialty stores

A convenient one-stop destination for daily essentials.

Oakfields Shopping Centre

A relaxed shopping centre on Hanekam Street offering:

  • Pick n Pay
  • Clicks
  • Specialty local stores

Ideal for quick and stress-free shopping.

Lakeside Mall

A large regional shopping mall situated on the banks of Civic Lake, with over 130 stores including:

  • Department stores
  • Restaurants
  • Banking facilities
  • Entertainment options

Perfect for a full shopping day.

✈️ Local Landmark — Airfield Heritage

Ovendales is located in the historic Airfield / Northmead area.

The area is known for its aviation heritage. Nearby guests may find scenic microlight flights, flight schools, aviation-related activities and a unique local character that gives the area its charm.
